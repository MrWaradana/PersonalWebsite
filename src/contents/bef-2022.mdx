## Short Description

Brebes Education Fair is the biggest student event in Brebes focused to prepare high school students for a college. Our main events are Try Out and College Expo.

## Background

I got invited by a friend to help him develop the website of Brebes Education Fair 2022. I was the front-end developer on this project and worked with other developers and designers as a web developer team. The website was developed to help the students to register for the event and to get information about the event.

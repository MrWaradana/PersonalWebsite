## Short Description

UKM Expo ITS 2022 is an annual event. This event is also a place for UKM ITS to introduce themselves to the new students of ITS.

## Background

UKM Expo ITS 2022 needed a website to introduce the event to the public. The website also needed to be able to display the UKM Expo ITS 2022 event schedule and the UKM Expo ITS 2022 event registration form and also a ticket purchase form for the closing event. For this year guest star is Payung Teduh Band.
